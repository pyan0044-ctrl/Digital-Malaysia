Section 02

The Social Media Boom

As Malaysians got online, they flocked to social media — peaking at 91.7% penetration in 2022. But not all platforms grew equally, as the final charts reveal.

Section 03

Getting Faster and More Mobile

Speed and mobile access tell a different side of Malaysia's digital story. By 2024, Malaysia had over 44 million mobile connections — more than its entire population. Mobile speeds have also grown nearly five times faster than fixed broadband since 2022, driven by the country's rapid 5G rollout.

Mobile vs Fixed Internet Speeds, 2022–2025
Median download speed in Mbps

Mobile speeds have grown nearly five times faster than fixed broadband since 2022 — a sign of Malaysia's rapid 5G rollout.

Source: Ookla via DataReportal
More SIMs Than People, 2020–2026
Mobile connections as % of total population

More SIMs than people — Malaysians often carry multiple SIM cards, pushing mobile connections well above the population count.

Source: DataReportal
